facilities in our school

library

there are many books

Our spacious library provides ample opportunities for our students to cultivate the reading habit. The school maintains a well equipped library and reading room. Books will be issued to students during the library periods. Books borrowed have to be returned on the 15th day after they are issued books/periodicals, once taken out of the library, if lost or damaged will have to be paid for.

science lab

scince lab

The school maintains well-equipped laboratories for Chemistry, Physics, Biology and Computer Science. The senior class students will have ample opportunities for doing experiments in all the branches of science. They shall maintain records of their observations of the experiments as per the prescribed syllabus, in their respective classes.

PARTS IN OUR SCHOOL

LITTLE FLOWER PLAY SCHOOL / MONFORT PLAY SCHOOL
for classes - Nursery , Lkg and Ukg

Little Flower Play School, Uppal, Hyderabad, established in June 2007, is managed by the Society of the Montfort Brothers of St. Gabriel. Catholic students receive religious instructions and all the others a course in Moral Science which instills in them values of their obligations to God, to themselves, to their country and their fellowmen

LITTLE FLOWER SCHOOL
for classes : I-X

Little Flower School, Uppal, Hyderabad, established in June 2007, is managed by the Society of the Montfort Brothers of St. Gabriel. It is recognized by the Government of Telangana and prepares boys and girls for the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) Examination is through the medium of English. Catholic students receive religious instructions and all the others a course in Moral Science which instills in them values of their obligations to God to themselves, to their country and their fellowmen.
